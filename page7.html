Children's Complaint
Center

Emotion is an indescribable psychological sensation, and children may have experienced their own psychological changes as they grow up, but they do not have enough expressive ability to describe their own situation. If you want to say something, it's always difficult to attract adults' attention when it comes to worrying things. Therefore, we use the "complaint center" as the medium and use "connection; trust; confide" as the keywords in our design to encourage children to draw or write about their troubles, vent their emotions, and display them in the form of journals, so that the voices of children in the community can be heard by more people.
